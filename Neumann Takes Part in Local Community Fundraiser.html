Neumann Takes Part in Local Community Fundraiser

SJNRA Students show appreciation for donations

Photo by Malissa Heim

Lucas Bower • Mar 22 2024

St. John Neumann Regional Academy participated in a community-wide fundraiser recently.

Raise the Region is an annual fundraising event for local organizations. The event raised $2,343,179 for 350 organizations.

Out of the 350 organizations, St. John Neumann placed 70th with most funds raised.

St. John Neumann had 26 donors with different goals to finance different projects such as helping families with tuition and to help rebuild the gym's concession stand.

Donors were able to pick different organizations to donate to.

Some other notable organizations that participated were Camp Susque, Central Pa Food Bank, Uptown Music YMCA, YWCA, SPCA, and Hope Enterprises.
