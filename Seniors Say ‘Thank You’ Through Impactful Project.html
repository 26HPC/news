Seniors Say ‘Thank You’ Through Impactful Project

The Senior Class of 2024 with the Neumann community members nominated for the project.

Photo by Albright Studio

Feb 09 2024

During Catholic Schools Week, each member of the Senior class of 2024 surprised a member of faculty, staff, or Neumann community figures who’ve made a specific impact on them.

Each senior gifted their nominee with a shirt that said “I Made An Impact” with each graduate’s last name on the back.

The nominees included teachers and athletic faculty representing various subjects and sports.
