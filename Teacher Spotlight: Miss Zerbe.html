Teacher Spotlight: Miss Zerbe

Photo by Photo by Albright Studio

Maggie Casale, Stella Kuri • Mar 22 2024

1. What do you like to do in your free time?

I like doing genealogy research. I like learning about where my family came from and how I am here where I am today.

2. What's the biggest challenge in the classroom and how do you overcome it?

The biggest challenge in my classroom is students talking. I overcome this by creating activities that include talking to each other.

3. What's your favorite topic to teach?

I like to teach how our government works because I like showing students how to get involved with their community. I also want them to know that they can change the world.
